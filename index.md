# ENGLISH

**Privacy Policy — Vi**
**Effective date:** 10.05.2026

## 1. Overview

Vi is a mobile application for personal conversations and biographical notes. We built Vi on one principle: your data belongs to you — and only you.

## 2. What data we collect

**We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers.**

The following occurs during use:

- Your messages are sent to the Anthropic API to generate Vi's responses. Anthropic does not store conversation content after processing. Details: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- Voice messages are sent to Google Cloud Speech-to-Text for recognition. Google does not store audio after processing.
- Authentication is handled via Google Sign-In. We receive only your name and email for display in the app.

## 3. Where your data is stored

All your conversations are stored **exclusively in your own Google Drive** — in the Vi_Archive folder. We have no access to these files after they are created. You can view, download, or delete them at any time directly through Google Drive.

## 4. Google Drive access

The app uses a limited OAuth scope `drive.file` — meaning Vi can only see files it created itself. It has no access to the rest of your Google Drive. You can revoke access at any time through your Google account settings.

## 5. Microphone

The app requests microphone access only when you press the record button. Audio is not recorded in the background. The temporary file is deleted from the device after recognition.

## 6. Analytics

We do not use analytics services and do not track your behavior in the app.

## 7. Your data rights

We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way. You have the right to delete all your data at any time by removing the Vi_Archive folder from your Google Drive and revoking app access in your Google account settings.

## 8. Children

Vi is not intended for persons under 13 years of age.

## 9. Changes

We will notify you of any changes via an app update.

## 10. Contact

Questions: pnalesnyk777@gmail.com

## 1. Descripción general

Vi es una aplicación móvil para conversaciones personales y notas biográficas. Construimos Vi con un principio: tus datos te pertenecen — y solo a ti.

**No recopilamos ni almacenamos tus datos personales en nuestros servidores.**

- Tus mensajes se envían a la API de Anthropic para generar las respuestas de Vi. Anthropic no almacena el contenido de las conversaciones tras el procesamiento. Más información: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

La app solicita acceso al micrófono solo cuando presionas el botón de grabación. El audio no se graba en segundo plano. El archivo temporal se elimina del dispositivo tras el reconocimiento.

No vendemos, compartimos ni monetizamos tus datos personales de ninguna manera. Tienes derecho a eliminar todos tus datos en cualquier momento — eliminando la carpeta Vi_Archive de Google Drive y revocando el acceso de la app en la configuración de tu cuenta de Google.
